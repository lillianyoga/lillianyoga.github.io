Bio

Lillian found yoga at an early age and immediately recognized its many benefits. She fell in love with the practice and the way it enhanced her other physical activities, such as mountaineering, rock and ice climbing, running, and tennis. She completed the 200-hour training at Yoga Vida in 2018 and further studied under the guidance of her mentor, Sangeeta Vallabhan.

Lillianâ€™s classes are athletic and challenging, yet balanced, safe, and accessible to all. She believes that a mindful yoga practice extends beyond the mat and infuses everything we do. With that in mind, Lillian aims to have people leave her studio feeling stronger, calmer, and more connected to the world around them.

In 2018, Lillian climbed Mount Everest, completing the Seven Summits. She has run the New York Marathon ten times, has qualified for and run the Boston Marathon twice, and regularly plays tennis. Likewise, she is a supporter and collector of the arts, and enjoys theater, dance and travel.

Yoga

Lillian took her first yoga class in 1980, and throughout the ensuing years, she has practiced many different styles of yoga, including vinyasa, iyengar, ashtanga, yin, integral, power, and restorative.

In 2018, Lillian chose to bring her love of yoga to the world and enrolled in the 200 Hour RYT Teacher Training at Yoga Vida in New York. She is continuing her training and will complete her 300 Hour RYT Teacher Training in 2020, following an extended study of the Bhagavad Gita in Vrindavan, India.

An avid mountain climber and competitive athlete, Lillian enjoys the phyical aspects of yoga and can provide a rigourous training session. However, her classes are very adaptable and appropriate for yogis of all experience levels. From nearly four decades of practice, Lillian has learned to modify her practice with age. She also specializes in trauma-informed yoga and can impart a restorative and respectful session.

Lillian can be found in yoga studios and gyms around New York City, and provides individual and small group sessions, as well. For more information, please get in touch.
